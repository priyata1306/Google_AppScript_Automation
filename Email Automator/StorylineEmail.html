What happens to your Storyline 2 courses?
Storyline 2 courses are compatible in Storyline 3. When you open an older course in Storyline 3, it will create a new version of the course, so the Storyline 2 version will not be affected.

To minimise any risk during your course development, we recommend that you complete any courses you currently have in production in Storyline 2, and then start brand new projects in Storyline 3.
