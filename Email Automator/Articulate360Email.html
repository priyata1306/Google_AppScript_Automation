Security is very important at PwC and becomes even more important when using online tools, such as GoMo. To reduce the risk of your accounts being hacked, you must create complex passwords that are at least eight characters long and contain alphabets and numbers. In addition, you must also change your password every 3 months. GoMo will not automatically prompt you to do this, so it is important that you do this yourself.
